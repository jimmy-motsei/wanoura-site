Who we are

From concept to final mix, we handle composition, sound design, VO direction, and post-production across campaigns, trailers, podcasts, and interactive work. Our approach blends artistic sensitivity with disciplined process—briefs are clarified, references are explored, and prototypes are tested early. We collaborate with agencies, directors, and product teams to solve for emotion, clarity, and impact; delivering masters engineered for broadcast, streaming, and multi-platform roll-outs.

What we do

We Design Sound that Moves

We craft original scores, immersive sound design, and branded audio systems that make messages memorable and experiences intuitive. From film and campaigns to products and podcasts, our work is built for clarity, emotion, and multi-platform delivery.

music with a heart beat

Scoring & Composition

We compose original themes, cues, and full scores that carry emotion and narrative. From subtle motifs to anthemic hooks, every piece is built around your story and audience. We deliver stems, alternates, and tempo-locked edits for seamless picture and platform integration.

Immersive Worlds

Sound Design & Audio Post

We craft textures, foley, atmos, and transitional soundscapes that place the listener inside the moment. Dialogue is cleaned, dynamics balanced, and mixes are mastered to spec—broadcast, cinema, streaming, and social—so your sound travels beautifully.

Human Connection Through Sound

Voice & Direction

We direct voice talent, cast the right voices for your brand, and guide performances that resonate. From commercial VO to podcast narration, we ensure every word carries the right tone, pace, and emotional weight for your audience.

Controlled Loudness, Confident Translation

Mixing & Mastering

We balance stems and dynamics for impact across cinemas, broadcast, and streaming. QC includes loudness, phase, and mono checks so your music and dialogue hold up on any system.
